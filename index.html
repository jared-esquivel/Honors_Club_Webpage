ABOUT

What is the Honors Club?

Open to all students

The Cabrillo Honors Club is student-run and separate from the Honors Transfer Program. You do not need to be in the Honors Program to join — all students are welcome.

Our mission

We help students explore advanced ideas, build community, and lead with empathy through collaboration, discussion, and service.

Goal

Our goal is to collaborate together and serve our community.

JOIN

Why you should join

Bring your interest in learning and your unique story. We value persistence, curiosity, and care for others as much as grades.

Joining the Honors Club connects you with motivated peers, workshops, research support, leadership opportunities, and a community that encourages you to learn, lead, and make an impact.
