How to Join the Honors Club

Applying is simple. Bring your interest in learning and your unique story. We care about curiosity, community, and growth.

1. Check eligibility

You do not need to be in the Honors Transfer Program to join. The club is open to students who want to get involved, grow as leaders, and support the Cabrillo community.

2. Submit your interest form

Fill out the interest form so officers can connect with you about meetings, events, volunteer opportunities, and club updates.
